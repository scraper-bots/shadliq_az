   - Correlation analysis shows price doesn't drive views

   - Complete all profile information (descriptions, hall names, services)
   - Encourage reviews and testimonials
   - Update content regularly
3. **Geographic Strategy**

   - If in high-density area (8 km): Strong differentiation needed
   - If in low-density area: Capture geographic monopoly
   - Consider accessibility and parking in marketing
4. **Revenue Diversification**

   - Don't rely solely on weddings
   - Target birthdays, corporate events, anniversaries
   - Develop weekday pricing strategies for corporate clients
5. **Communication Channels**

   - Phone and messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram) are primary

   - Fast response times critical for booking conversions

1. **Market Entry Options**:

   - **Option A**: Join established clusters (8 km) - benefit from traffic, face competition

   - Unique architectural design

3. **Marketing Priorities**:

   - Professional photography from day one

### For Investors:

1. **Market Characteristics**:

   - Geographic clustering suggests network effects
2. **Investment Thesis**:

   - High-view venues with low prices: Potential for price optimization

3. **Due Diligence Focus**:
